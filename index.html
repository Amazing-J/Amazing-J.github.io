Bio

Jie Zhu received his M.S. degree from Soochow University, China, in 2021. He has been working at Alibaba Group since then as a large language model algorithm expert in the Qwen Dianjin Team. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D., with research interests in large language models and their applications in the financial domain.

Research Interests

  • Large Language Models
  • Natural Language Processing (various tasks)
  • Financial AI & financial text understanding
